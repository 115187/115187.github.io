SLOTH INFORMATION AND FACTS

What are sloths?
Sloths are amazing and unique slow-moving animals that are known to live in the large forests of South America.

WHAT SLOTHS DO
Sloths spend most of their lives Sleeping- they usually sleep 10-20 hours a day- and eating, however, they also spend some of their time swimming. Since sloths are only awake a few hours a day, they don't spend too much time moving around, so they usually spend most of their lives living in the same tree.

DIET

Two-toed sloths are omnivores and tend to eat things that grow on trees like leaves and shoots, but are also known to eat small lizards, and other things. Three-toed sloths are herbivores, so they they only eat plants and greens.

FUN FACTS:

-Sloths are so slow that they can grow algae on their fur.

-Sloths' predators includde eagles, snakes, jaguars and anything willing to eat them

-Sloths have long claws to help them climb, hang and protect themselves from predators
